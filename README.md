# 🚌 City Bus Parking Simulator

A 3D physics-based driving and parking simulation game developed in Unity. The player is tasked with navigating a city environment, collecting coins, and completing a highly precise parking challenge.

## 🎮 Gameplay & Mechanics

* **Physics-Based Driving:** Custom Rigidbody-based bus controller for realistic acceleration, turning, and solid collision detection.
* **Dynamic Mission System:** Players must explore the map and collect 100 coins. The final parking zone only unlocks after this objective is completed.
* **Precision Parking Algorithm:** The parking system is designed to prevent "cheating." It calculates the exact geometric center distance and rotation angle of the bus. The player must align the bus perfectly within the zone and remain completely stationary for 2 seconds to win.
* **Dynamic Audio:** Engine sounds that react to player input, shifting pitch realistically between idle and acceleration states.

## 🛠️ Technologies Used
* **Engine:** Unity 6
* **Language:** C#
* **Core Systems:** Unity Physics (Rigidbody, Colliders), UI Canvas, Scene Management.
